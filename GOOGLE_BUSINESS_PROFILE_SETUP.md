## ‚úÖ Step 8: Verification Process

### Verification Methods (Google will choose):
1. **Postcard Verification** (Most Common)
   - Google sends a postcard to your business address
   - Contains a verification code
   - Arrives in 5-7 business days
   - Enter code in GBP dashboard

2. **Phone Verification**
   - Google calls your business number
   - Provides verification code
   - Enter code immediately

3. **Email Verification**
   - Google sends email with verification link
   - Click link to verify

4. **Video Verification** (For some businesses)
   - Live video call with Google representative
   - Show your business location and operations

### After Verification:
- Your profile will be live on Google Search and Maps
- You can start receiving and responding to reviews
- Your business will appear in local search results

## üö® Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Can't find my business to claim
**Solution**: 
- Search with different variations of your business name
- Search by address only
- Create a new profile if business doesn't exist

### Issue: Verification postcard not arriving
**Solution**:
- Wait 14 business days
- Check address is correct
- Contact Google Business Profile support
- Request new verification code

### Issue: Duplicate listings
**Solution**:
- Claim all duplicate listings
- Mark duplicates for removal
- Keep the most complete listing active
- Contact Google support if needed

### Issue: Reviews not showing
**Solution**:
- Reviews may take 24-48 hours to appear
- Ensure reviews comply with Google's policies
- Check if reviews are being filtered
- Contact Google support if reviews disappear

### Issue: Low visibility in search
**Solution**:
- Complete all profile sections (100% complete)
- Get more reviews (aim for 20+)
- Post regularly (weekly)
- Add more photos
- Ensure NAP consistency across web
